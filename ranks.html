ABOUT US

Mykal's MC Server is a Fabric 1.19.2 Survival style server. We require you to have the Create Mod and Oh The Biomes You'll Go Refabricated, along with their respective dependencies in order to login. [TerraBlender (Fabric) and FabricAPI]. It is also recommended to grab JEI (Just Enough Items) in order to view the Create Mod recipes. We are currently looking for Mods to help with the server.
